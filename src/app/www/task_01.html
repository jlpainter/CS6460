Task 1: Analysis

Working with your partner(s), investigate whether the various chicken feed brands have any effect on the growth of chickens.

Research has shown that chickens are usually around 27.6 inches tall and weigh approximately 2.6 kg (5.7 pounds) on average. However, various chicken feed producers have made bold claims that their brand of chicken feed can help you grow even larger chickens! We need to setup some experiments to test those claims, and help farmer Billy decide whether or not it is worth the added expense to switch from his current chicken feed to one of these more expensive brands.
